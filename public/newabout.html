WHO WE ARE

The J.K.U.A.T. Society of Engineering Students (S.E.S.) is a body established for the student who desires to discover what lies beyond books. S.E.S.'s activities are geared towards holistic development of its members, molding individuals who are not only well-adapted at classroom level, but also stand out in the practical scene and corporate engineering world at large.

SES ANNUAL EVENTS

1 / 3

Dinner

This is an event organized to enable the engineering students to engage with each other and have some fun as they eat a palatable meal. The main agenda of the night is usually to hand over the mantle to the next executive for the next regime. The new executive is usually sworn in to leadership on the same day.

2 / 3

WIT Picnic

This is a get together amongst female engineering students to share ideas in the field and upcoming trends in tech.It’s also an opportunity to network witheach other and at the same time have fun and bond.The students and usually encouraged to participate in hackathons organized by the committee.

3 / 3

Techweek

This is an event organized for students to provide an opportunity for them to learn as well as to network with industry professionals. It’s a two day event which consists of workshops and hackathons on the first and second day respectively. The hackathons are inclusive of all the engineering fields.
