#1 JKUAT Society of engineering students annual dinner

A dinner for JKUAT Society of Engineering Students

This is a dinner for JKUAT Society of Engineering Students which will be held on 23rd December 2023 at GS Grand Hotel, Thika Super Highway, Kenyatta Road. The dinner will start at 4pm and end at 9pm. The dinner will be a celebration of the society's achievements and a way to appreciate the students who have been working hard to make the society a success. There will be awards given to the best performing students in the society.
